This ranking is the core sector risk view for credit analysis. Agriculture, Forestry And Fishing is currently the highest-risk sector at 3.50, while Transport, Postal And Warehousing is lowest at 2.14. These scores combine public ABS/RBA signals with public-data-derived classification proxies.
